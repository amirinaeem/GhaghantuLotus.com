About Our Mission

Educate Her was founded in 2015 with the mission to eliminate gender disparity in education in rural communities. We believe every girl deserves access to quality education regardless of her geographical location or economic background.

To date, we've helped over 5,000 girls attend school, provided scholarships to 1,200 students, and built 15 schools in underserved communities.
